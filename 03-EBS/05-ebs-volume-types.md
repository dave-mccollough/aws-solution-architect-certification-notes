# EBS Volume Types

- EBS Volumes come in 6 different types
  - gp2/gp3 (SSD)
    - General purpose SSD volume
    - Balances price and performance
    - Good for a variety of workloads
    - Can be used as a boot volume
      - Virtual desktops, development and test enviornments
  - io 1/io 2 (SSD)
    - Highest performance SSD
    - Use for mission critical low latency or high throughput workloads
    - Can be used as a boot volume
  - st | (HDD)
    - Low cost HDD volume
    - Designed for frequently accessed, throughput intensive workloads
  - sc | (HDD)
    - Lowest cost HDD volume
    - Designed for less frequently accessed workloads
